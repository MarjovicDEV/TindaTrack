# TindaTrack - Utang Recording System

## Overview
TindaTrack is a simple system designed to help sari-sari store owners record and manage customer utang (credit purchases). The system keeps track of customer balances, payment history, and product purchases to make store management easier and more organized.

---

# Features

## Utang Recording
- Record customer utang
- Add purchased items
- Save total amount owed
- Add date and time of transaction
- View transaction history

## Payment Management
- Record partial payments
- Record full payments
- Automatically update remaining balance
- View payment history

## Dashboard
- Display total unpaid balances
- Show recently added utang
- Show paid and unpaid transactions

## Reports
- Generate daily reports
- Generate monthly reports
- View customer balance summaries
- Monitor inventory movement reports

## Inventory Management
- Add new products
- Update product stock
- Monitor low-stock items
- Track sold products
- View available inventory
- Automatically reduce stock when products are added to utang
- Manage product categories and prices

---

# System Modules

## 1. Login Module
Allows store owners or staff to securely log in to the system.

---

## 2. Customer Module
Used for managing customer information.

### Customer Information
- Customer Name
- Contact Number
- Address

---

## 3. Utang Module
Used for recording credit purchases.

### Utang Details
- Customer Name
- Products Purchased
- Quantity
- Total Amount
- Date Borrowed
- Due Date
- Status (Paid / Unpaid)

---

## 4. Inventory Module
Used for managing store products and stocks.

### Inventory Details
- Product Name
- Product Category
- Stock Quantity
- Product Price
- Expiration Date (optional)
- Supplier Information

---

# Sample Workflow

1. Store owner logs in.
2. Select or add customer.
3. Record products bought on credit.
4. Save transaction.
5. Customer balance is automatically updated.
6. Product inventory is automatically reduced.
7. When customer pays, payment is recorded.
8. Remaining balance is updated.
